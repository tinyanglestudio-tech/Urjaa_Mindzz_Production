Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

At Urjaa Mindz, we collect information you provide when joining our community, subscribing to our newsletter, or registering for workshops — including your name, email, and your child's age group for personalisation.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to personalise content, send workshop reminders, share weekly wisdom resources, and improve our platform. We never sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We use reputable third-party services for secure storage.

4. Cookies & Analytics

We use analytics tools to understand how our community uses the platform so we can improve the experience. You can opt out at any time through your browser settings.

5. Children's Privacy

Our platform is designed for parents and caregivers. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. Any child-related information (such as age group) is provided by parents for content personalisation only.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. Contact us at privacy@urjaamindz.com.

7. Contact

Questions? Reach us at our contact page or email urjjakids@gmail.com.
